 BMFC 

 The Blue Mountains Football Club, is a very friendly and competitive organisation. 

 BMFC U16 is for under 16 boys to be able to sign up/register and play soccer. Soccer   games will be held every Saturday (times and fields/locations vary). Training will be   held every Thursday, times vary, located at the Knapsack Park/field.
